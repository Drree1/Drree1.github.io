Problem Statement: Healthcare System

Our countrys healthcare and emergency services system is something that needs improving. Whenever someone has to go to the hospital they have to fill out multiple sheets of information, and then wait hours to even see a doctor. This not only puts peoples health at risk, but it also creates agitation in the emergency room which is the last place its needed. Our plan is to create an app to allow people to check in and fill out all necessary paperwork, so that once they arrive at the hospital they can go straight to the back and/or reduce their waiting time significantly.
